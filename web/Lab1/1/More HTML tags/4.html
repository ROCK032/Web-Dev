Pasta Carbonara

  • Time: 25 minutes
  • Serves: 2 people
Ingredients Quantity
Pasta 200 g
Bacon 100 g
Eggs 2
Cheese 50 g

Step 1: Boil the pasta in salted water.

Step 2: Fry the bacon until crispy.

Step 3: Mix eggs and cheese, then combine everything together.

Source: BBC Good Food

Pancakes

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 3 people
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 1 cup
Milk 1 cup
Egg 1
Sugar 2 tbsp

Step 1: Mix all ingredients together.

Step 2: Pour batter into a hot pan.

Step 3: Cook until golden on both sides.

Source: AllRecipes
